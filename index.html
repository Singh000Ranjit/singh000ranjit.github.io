WELCOME TO MY HOMEPAGE OF USEFUL CALCULATORS

Hello! I have made two calculators, one for pay arrears for pspcl employees and one for voltage drop, which may be useful for pspcl employees/junior engineers to get the rough idea.

My Projects

  • PSPCL DUE DRAWN PAY ARREAR CALCULATOR (01.01.2016 - 30.06.2021) -This web-based calculator is a specialized financial tool designed exclusively for the employees of the Punjab State Power Corporation Limited (PSPCL). Its primary purpose is to automate the complex and meticulous calculation of salary arrears owed to an employee for the period spanning January 1, 2016, to June 30, 2021. This is for information purpose only. Utmost care has been taken in designing, however, there is no gurantee of accuracy.
  • 11 kV FEEDER VOLTAGE DROP CALCULATOR - This is a self-contained calculator, designed for electrical engineers and technicians to calculate the voltage drop across 11 kV electrical feeders. The calculator is based on the official standards outlined in "S.I. No. 7, Date-26.3.92 - UPDATED STANDARDISATION INSTRUCTION NO.7".
